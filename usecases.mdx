# Use Cases

Zaymo offers several integration options to enhance your email marketing and customer experience. Each use case addresses specific business needs and can be implemented independently or in combination.

## Product Upsells {#product-upsells}

The Product Upsells integration allows your customers to add products directly from an email without leaving their inbox.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-upsell-example.gif" alt="Product upsell example" />
  *Example of a product upsell in email*
</Frame>

### How It Works

1. Customer receives an email with embedded product options
2. Customer selects a product and clicks "Add to Cart"
3. The product is instantly added to their account
4. Customer receives confirmation without ever leaving their email

### Required Endpoints

| Endpoint | Purpose | Required |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Get Products](/api-reference/upsells/get-products) | Retrieve product information to display in email | Yes |
| [Add Product](/api-reference/upsells/add-product) | Process the customer's product selection | Yes |
| [Customer Info](/api-reference/upsells/customer-info) | Retrieve customer-specific information | Optional |

### Required Data

- **Product Information**:
  - Product ID
  - Title
  - Price (before discount)
  - Image URLs
  - Description (optional)
  - Variants (if applicable)
- **Customer Information**:
  - Customer ID
  - One-time purchase flag
  - Discount code (optional)

## Portal {#portal}

The Portal integration allows subscribers to manage their subscriptions directly from email, including rescheduling deliveries or requesting immediate shipment.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/portal-example.gif" alt="Subscription portal example" />
  *Example of subscription management in email*
</Frame>

### How It Works

1. Subscriber receives an email with subscription management options
2. Subscriber can view current subscription details
3. Subscriber can reschedule their next delivery or request immediate shipment
4. Changes are confirmed without leaving the email

### Required Endpoints

| Endpoint | Purpose | Required |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Subscription Info](/api-reference/portal/subscription-info) | Retrieve current subscription details | Yes |
| [Reschedule](/api-reference/portal/reschedule) | Process subscription rescheduling requests | Yes |
| [Send Now](/api-reference/portal/send-now) | Process immediate shipment requests | Yes |

### Required Data

- **Subscription Information**:
  - Subscription ID
  - Current delivery schedule
  - Product details
- **Rescheduling Information**:
  - Customer ID
  - New delivery date
- **Send Now Information**:
  - Customer ID
  - Subscription ID

## Reactivate {#reactivate}

The Reactivate integration makes it easy for customers to reactivate cancelled subscriptions directly from email.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/reactivate-example.gif" alt="Reactivation example" />
  *Example of subscription reactivation in email*
</Frame>

### How It Works

1. Former subscriber receives an email with reactivation offer
2. Customer views their cancelled subscription details
3. Customer clicks to reactivate their subscription, potentially with a special offer
4. Subscription is reactivated without leaving the email

### Required Endpoints

| Endpoint | Purpose | Required |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [List Cancelled Subscriptions](/api-reference/reactivate/list-cancelled) | Retrieve cancelled subscription details | Yes |
| [Reactivate](/api-reference/reactivate/reactivate) | Process subscription reactivation | Yes |

### Required Data

- **Cancelled Subscription Information**:
  - Customer ID
  - Subscription details
- **Reactivation Information**:
  - Customer ID
  - Discount code (optional)
